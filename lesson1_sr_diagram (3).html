Support & Resistance

The floor and the ceiling — click a button to isolate each concept

Resistance ($65)
Support ($50)
Price action
Support — the floor. Notice how price drops towards $50 and bounces back up each time. Buyers are waiting at that level — they see it as good value, so demand increases and price reverses. The more times price bounces off a level, the stronger it becomes.
Resistance — the ceiling. Every time price reaches $65, sellers step in and push it back down. They've decided that's a fair price to sell at — so supply increases and the rally stalls. This level acts like a roof that the price keeps hitting its head on.
